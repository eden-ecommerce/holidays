Key formulae:   **Western Easter:** Use the Anonymous Gregorian algorithm (RFC 5545). Implemented in most date libraries — e.g. date-holidays npm package provides this natively. **Orthodox Pascha:** Same algorithm applied to Julian calendar, then converted to Gregorian. Orthodox Pascha \= Julian Easter \+ 13 days (Gregorian equivalent). Exception: if this falls before or on Western Easter, add 7 days. **Armenian Easter:** Same as Western Easter. **Coptic / Ethiopian Easter:** Same algorithm as Orthodox (Julian-based). Note: Ethiopian calendar year is different but the Gregorian output date coincides with Orthodox Pascha in most years. **Ash Wednesday:** Easter − 46 days **Pentecost:** Easter \+ 49 days **Ascension:** Easter \+ 39 days (Thursday) — note many Western countries have transferred to following Sunday (Easter \+ 42\) **Trinity Sunday:** Easter \+ 56 days **Corpus Christi:** Easter \+ 60 days (Thursday) or \+ 63 days (Sunday, in England & Wales) **Christ the King:** The Sunday falling on or before 26 November (last Sunday before Advent) **First Sunday of Advent:** Nearest Sunday to November 30 (between Nov 27 and Dec 3\) **Vardavar (Armenian):** Easter \+ 98 days   **9\.
